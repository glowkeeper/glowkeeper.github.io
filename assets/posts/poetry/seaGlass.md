# Sea Glass

She swims and then finds,
Sea glass from Southwick,
On the shore strandline,
And occasionally,
She also happens,
To see a migrant,
Declined, by stormy,
Wild, easterlies,
Hunger appeased,
By rewards found picking,
At the shore strandline,
Just like my girlfriend.

**14 October, 2023**
