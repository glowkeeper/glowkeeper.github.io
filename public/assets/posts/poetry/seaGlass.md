# Sea Glass

She swims and then finds,
On the shore strandline,
Sea glass from Southwick.
And occasionally,
She also happens,
To see - a migrant,
Declined, by stormy,
Wild, easterlies.
Their hunger appeased,
By rewards they find,
At the shore strandline,
Just like my girlfriend.

**14 October, 2023**
